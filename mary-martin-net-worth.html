FAQs About Mary Martin

Who is Mary Martin?

Mary Martin was an American actress and singer known for her iconic roles in musical theater. She’s best remembered for her performances in Peter Pan, The Sound of Music, and South Pacific.

When was she born?

She was born on December 1, 1913, in Weatherford, Texas. This small-town girl went on to become a Broadway legend.

What is she most famous for?

She’s most famous for playing Peter Pan in the 1954 Broadway production. Her performance was so beloved that she reprised the role in multiple televised productions.

Did she win any awards?

Absolutely! She won three Tony Awards for Best Actress in a Musical for her roles in South Pacific, Peter Pan, and The Sound of Music. She also received a special Tony Award in 1977.
