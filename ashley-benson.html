Ashley Benson News - Us Weekly

Name:Ashley Benson

Hometown:Anaheim Hills, CA

Birthday:December 18, 1989

About

Ashley Benson is perhaps best known for her starring roles on Days of Our Lives and Pretty Little Liars. Born December 18, 1989, in Long Beach, California, Benson signed with Ford Modeling Agency when she was 8 years old.

After several commercials and one-time TV appearances, Benson landed the role of Abigail Deveraux on Days of Our Lives in 2004. Before long, she appeared on shows such as 7th Heaven, Zoey 101, Eastwick and The OC, as well as the 2007 movie Bring It On: In It to Win It.

Benson’s breakout role came in 2010 upon appearing as Hanna Marin in Freeform’s Pretty Little Liars, based on the book series by Sara Shepard. She starred opposite Lucy Hale, Troian Bellisario and Shay Mitchell on the teen mystery.

From there, Benson continued to land roles in movies including Spring Breakers, Pixels and Elvis & Nixon.

Over the years, Benson has been romantically linked to One Call singer Justin “JJ” Thorne, Ryan Good, Chord Overstreet and James Franco. However, her most high-profile romance came in 2018 when she began dating Cara Delevingne. The pair called it quits in May 2020 after two years of dating, and Benson started seeing G-Eazy shortly after. Benson and the rapper dated on and off until early 2023 before she moved on with Brandon Davis. Davis proposed in July 2023.
