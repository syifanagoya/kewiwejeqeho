Cupcakes And Cashmere Creator, Emily Schuman Married Her Husband And Shares A Daughter

An author, Emily Schuman, is the creator of a Cupcakes and Cashmere, a blog that mainly focuses on fashion, food, beauty, and design. She also released the Cupcakes and Cashmere book, which is a complete guide for Defining Style. 

Besides, Emily also launched Shop Cupcakes and Cashmere which carries gifts, clothing lines, hand-selected apparel, and jewelry as well. 

Emily Schuman Married Life 

Famous blogger, author, Emily shares her wedding vows with Geoffrey Fuller in the presence of seventy-eight guests including friends and families. Moreover, the couple looks stunning in their big day; bride opted for sleeveless mermaid gown and groom chooses black tuxedo along with a black bow.

In 2007, Fuller first proposed Schuman when she was 24. Afterward, she shares the good news with her mother during face-timed, and her mom approval even strengthened their love life. 

After three years of romance, the couple finally engaged on December 2010. Since then, the love birds are together without any separation rumors.

Mother To Sloan Fuller

The couple is blessed parents of a beautiful daughter named Sloan Fuller born on February 6, 2015. Emily believes, the birth of her little one brings happiness with her, which completed the family of three.

Enjoying parenthood for four years now, Mr. and Mrs. Schuman often shares her kid pictures on social sites. Interestingly, unlike other celebrity parents, they are quite open about their daughter social interaction.

Cup-Cakes and Cashmere Creator 

In March 2008, Emily created Cup-cakes and Cashmere. This name is based on the topic of fashion, beauty, travel, food, and decor. Firstly, Emily made a blog which includes travel food and lifestyle. 

After nine years of blog journey, she inflates Cupcakes and Cashmere as an online shop, which carries her clothing line, jewelry, gifts, and many more.

Then in August 2012, she launched her very first book 'Cupcakes and Cashmere: a guide to define the style, entertaining with ease, and reinventing your fashion.' The book became a national bestseller and Emily also rose to the limelight. 

After a grand success from her first book, in 2015 she published her second book named; 'Cupcakes and Cashmere at Home.'

Total Fortune and Earnings

Emily's total net worth is around $1 million, which is comparable to another American author, Janet Mock. According to Payscale, the average pay of an author in America is $50,993 per year, so, she might earn the same figure.

Also, she has her own online shopping store, and blog from there too, she is garnering a huge worth. Her wage can be comparable to another blogger Melissa Wood, who earns $39,000 annually. 

Fuller family is now residing at a 1,800 square feet house in a Los Angeles, California which price must be more than $1 million. The luxurious house consists of open-plan space with three bedrooms, two bathrooms, a dining room, and a living room among many others. Moreover, the rooms are decorated with 'Pelican Gray' and 'Romantic Pink' colors.
