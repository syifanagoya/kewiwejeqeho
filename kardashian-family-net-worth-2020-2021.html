Everything about Kardashians

Everything was started with Late Robert Kardashian, who was an American attorney. He became famous in 1995 for defending his client O.J. Simpson in a murder trial. Although Robert won the case, it is till to his day one of the most controversial verdicts in American history.

Before he became famous from the O.J. Simpson case, Robert was married to Kris Kardashian (now a Jenner); the couple had four children – Kim, Kourtney, Khloé, and Robert Jr. In 1991 the couple got divorced three years before the trial actually happened. In 2003, Robert Kardashian passed away, weeks after being diagnosed with esophageal cancer.

The Kardashian family was in the spotlight during the O.J. Simpson trial. The media was all over Robert, and his ex-wife and their children were also dragged into the. Although Robert Kardashian passed away in 2003, the family rise to fame seemed just to have started.

Kris Jenner was the mastermind behind the Kardashian family’s success. She was the woman that made this all possible. Kris married and divorced Robert Kardashian and then was remarried with Bruce Jenner, with whom she had two more children, Kendall Jenner and Kylie Jenner. During Kris’s marriage to Bruce Jenner, Kris managed the careers of all of her six children.

But in December 2014, Her relationship with Bruce ended, after officially getting divorced. Kris started dated Corey Gamble for two years. Corey appeared on the family’s show, and was very close to the Kardashians, especially Kourtney and her kids. Fans of the show became sad by the news of their breakup, but they have since been seen back together.

Caitlyn’s relationship with the Kardashians has now changed. The family feud increased after Caitlyn released a memoir that shows Kris Jenner arguing, and it portrayed her in a negative light.
