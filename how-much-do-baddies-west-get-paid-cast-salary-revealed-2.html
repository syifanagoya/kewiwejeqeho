How Much Do Baddies West Get Paid? Cast Salary Revealed

Baddies West get paid $5000 per season from the show. The third season of Baddies premiered on January 22, 2023, on Zeus Network.

The first season of the show, Baddie ATL, debuted on May 16, 2021. the reality series was developed following an episode of Zeus’s The Conversation, which featured cast members of Bad Girls Club.

Following its popularity, the show was renewed for a 14-episode second series, Baddies South, which aired from June 12, 2022, to September 18, 2022.

On September 24, 2022, creator Natalie Nunn announced she was going to commence casting for a third season, Baddies West, which dropped in January 2023 with 11 cast members.

This season the cast members will travel to various locations such as Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Phoenix, Oakland, and Portland.

The cast includes Natalie Nunn, Catya Washington, Loren Jordan, Tommie Lee, Gia “Rollie” Mayham, Chrisean Malone, Scotlynd “Scotty” Ryan, Biggie, Stunna Girl, Monique “Razor”, and DJ Sky High Baby.

How Much Do Baddies West Get Paid?

Baddies West cast get paid $5000 from appearing in the show. The pay may vary following their contribution to the series.

Reality TV stars share their life details while being in front of the camera. This often draws viewers’ curiosity to know about their earnings. They question how the show compensates for their time.

When an individual signs up to be on a reality show, they aren’t just dedicating a few hours of their day that will air on primetime. Rather they agree to 24/7 access to their private space.

Zeus Network, a subscription-based video streaming service, features a variety of original scripted and unscripted shows on its channel.

Unscripted reality shows include Baddies and its all follow-up seasons, Pretty Girls Lit, A Taste of Dance with Amanda Cerny, The Conversation, Doses of Draya, and more.

The stars of various shows are paid accordingly based on their time and contribution. Reportedly, the Zeus Network pays a similar amount to its reality stars which range from $2,000 to $10,000 per show.

Talking about other channel’s reality shows, TLC’s 90 Day Fiance pays their stars $1,000 to $1,500 per episode (per couple), and ABC’s The Bachelor reportedly pays their cast members $70,000 to $100,000 per season.

Similarly, Teen Mom’s stars have an estimated salary of around $25,000 per episode. Some sources suggest the original Teen Moms (say Catelyn Lowell) may earn at minimum $500,000 per season.

What Is Baddies Club Salary?

Regular Baddies cast certainly have higher salaries compared to newcomers. They also earn more in Season 3 than they did in Season 1.

Nunn and Tanisha Thomas created Baddies, following an episode of The Conversation. The episode featured stars from the former Oxygen series Bad Girls Club, created by Jonathan Murray.

Reports say each cast member of Bad Girls Club received $500 weekly (approximately). However, the show did not cater to expenses like random outings and lunches.

Coming to the Baddies cast, Tanisha Thomas and Nunn are believed to have made around $10,000 from the first show, as they were the executive producers.

Sarah Oliver and Christina Salgado earned around $5,000 because they were producers. Salgado appeared as a main cast in the first two seasons. Oliver was a main cast in Season 1 and appeared as a guest star in Season 2.

The other girls probably got a salary somewhere between $2,000 to $3,000.

Currently, Nun makes between $10,000 to $15,000, while the remaining cast members make slightly more than before.

One curious Reddit user asked if the amount refers to total earning or their pay per episode. Another user replied, “Definitely not per episode. I believe it’s total.”

They added, “Zeus doesn’t seem like they have a big budget.” “Oh okay that makes sense,” the first user responded.

Who Has The Highest Net Worth?

Natalie Nunn is considered to be the richest Baddies cast member with a net worth of $1 million. Reality stars earn from various sources such as social media, business, and other endeavors.

Here is the list of some of the wealthiest Baddies stars according to their net worth.

Nunn is a prominent American reality TV star

Bad Girls Club Season 4 star Natalie Nunn has a whopping net worth of $1 million. Besides Season 4, she also appeared in Season 13 of the reality series.

Moreover, she appeared in Hair Battle Spectacular and was a contestant on Season 2 of Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too. She then participated in Bad Girls All-Star Battle Season 1.

Back in 2012, she was featured on Oxygen’s Tanisha Gets Married as a Bridesmaid at her friend and fellow Bad Girl cast’s wedding, along with Amber Meade and Florina Kaja.

She has participated in several WeTv shows, including Marriage Boot Camp. Nunn also starred in Lifetime’s Mother/Daughter Experiment with her mom, Karen Nunn.

Her television career and partnership with multiple luxury brand sponsors have been profitable. She adds extra fortune to her income through sponsored postings, business collaborations, and participation in interviews.

Baddies Razor is a businesswoman

Razor from Baddies West has an estimated net worth of $1 million. She makes her money through her two salons and a clothing brand.

She has a similar income as Nunn, the executive producer of Baddies and the Bad Boys. Monique Razor identifies herself as a businesswoman and is the owner of Razor Behavior Studios.

The salon specializes in waves and cuts. They offer three categories in hair color -full hair costs $275 per hour, highlights cost $225 for 50 minutes, and partial for $185.

Besides the salon, she owns a clothing brand, Major Collections Boutique. They provide different brands of clothing from the low price of $25 to a high $52.

Biggie is an event planner

Baddies Biggie has a net worth of $500 thousand. Biggie makes her money as a reality television star and an event planner.

The 26-year-old reality star is known as one of the famous event planners from Rhode Island. Her Instagram bio says there’s nothing that moves the city without her.

Her events ticket price ranges from $30 to $150. She recently hosted an event, Set It Off Fridays, on February 17.

She spends most of her income on luxury clothes and is seen wearing branded clothes everywhere she goes. Her Instagram showcases her daily lifestyle and how she spends her hard-earned fortune.

Baddies fans love her from her audition as she kept it real about who she is. She flew six hours to audition for the show and yes, it was definitely worth it.
