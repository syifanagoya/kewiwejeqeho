Who is Michael Rapaport?

He is a well-known actor and comedian from New York City. Born on March 20, 1970, he’s rocked the screen in both TV and movies, known for his sharp wit and everyman screen presence.

What movies and TV shows has he been in?

He has a pretty extensive resume. You might recognize him from TV shows like The War at Home, Boston Public, and Prison Break. On the big screen, he’s appeared in films like True Romance, Higher Learning, and The Heat.

Does he have a podcast?

Yes, he does! He hosts the I Am Rapaport podcast where he dishes on everything from sports and entertainment to current events, all sprinkled with his trademark humor.

Is Michael Rapaport married?

Yes, he is married. He married actress Kebe Dunn in 2016. Prior to that, he was married to writer and producer Nichole Beattie from 2000 until they divorced in 2007.

While Michael hasn’t snagged any of the major acting awards like an Oscar or Emmy, his performances have been consistently praised, and he’s a beloved figure in the entertainment industry for his dynamic roles.
