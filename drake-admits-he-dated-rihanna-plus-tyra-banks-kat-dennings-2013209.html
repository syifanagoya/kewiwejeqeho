Drake Dating History: Confirms Rihanna, Tyra Banks, Kat Dennings Dates

Drake‘s dating diary! The “Started from the Bottom” rapper, 26, was in the hot seat chatting about his romantic history on the Friday, Sept. 20 episode of Ellen — and Drizzy was surprisingly frank!

As Ellen DeGeneres subjected the Grammy winner to a rapid-fire Q&A — displaying photos of famous women — Drake obliged with the details on each. Rihanna? “Yeah,” he confirmed of Chris Brown‘s ex. “Great girl. We had our moment. Always support and have love for her and yeah . . . ”

Nicki Minaj? “Me and Nicki are like family,” he explained of his frequent collaborator. “I just love Nicki with all my heart. We’ve haven’t really shared like intimate moments.”

Tyra Banks: “I went on a date with her one time, yeah. We went to Disneyland in disguise actually, which was fun,” he said of the supermodel, 39. “I don’t know if it was a date. It was a get together. We’re close as well.”

As Drake grew increasingly flustered, DeGeneres showed him Kanye West‘s love, Kim Kardashian. “Oh no, no. What’s going on here?”
