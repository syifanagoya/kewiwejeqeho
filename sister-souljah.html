Who is Sister Souljah:

Sister Souljah is a famous Novelist. She was born on January 28, 1964 and her birthplace is The Bronx, NY. Sister is also well known as, Born Lisa Williamson, her wide-ranging career has seen her as a recording artist, music executive, novelist, film producer and activist. Her remarks about race during the 1992 presidential campaign that Bill Clinton would publicly criticize gained her prominence. That moment gave birth to the term “Sister Souljah Moment” in politics.

Sister is originated from United States. She was the executive director of the Sean Combs and Bad Boy Entertainment financed non-profit for urban youth called Daddy’s House Social Programs Inc.
