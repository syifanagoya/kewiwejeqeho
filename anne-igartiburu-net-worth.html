Career Achievements

Anne started her career in local television in Mondragón in 1993. She then moved to Euskal Telebista and later to Telecinco, where she co-hosted Una pareja feliz in the 1994-95 season.

However, it was her role as the host of Corazón, starting in 1997, that made her a household name.

She also hosted the Spanish version of Dancing with the Stars, called ¡Mira quién baila!, from 2005 to 2009. Additionally, Anne co-hosted the Gala 50 años de TVE in 2006, celebrating the 50th anniversary of TVE.

Personal Life

Anne’s personal life is as interesting as her career. She was married to Igor Yebra, a ballet dancer, from 2004 to 2006. She has two adopted daughters: Noa, born in India in 2000, and Carmen, born in Vietnam in 2011.

In 2015, Anne married Pablo Heras-Casado, a renowned conductor. The couple welcomed their son, Nicolás, in 2016.
